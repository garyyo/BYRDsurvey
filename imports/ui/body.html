You will be given the PIE separated and the Germanic Separated forms of a word. If it is correct, select correct and move on to the next. Make sure that what you have written is correct and hit next. You cannot go back to previous entries to correct them.

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to collect data to train an Artificial Intelligence to be able to give reasons as to why a change is wrong or why it is right.
